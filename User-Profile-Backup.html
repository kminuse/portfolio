Introduction

The present project was developed and executed in collaboration with Ugam Kumar and Vane Georgiev. The aim of this survey was to understand what user personalization means for each and every team at Onefootball. So to discover that, participants were asked to rate how helpful it would be, to know certain user attributes, in order to create a personalized experience for our user. More than that, we wanted to understand how each team would make use of these data.

Methodology & Engagement

We have conducted a survey, in order to collect responses from 30 different teams at Onefootball, including Management. There were both, slider questions (7 scale) and open questions. Almost every team had at least one representative that has completed the survey. We had a 57% completion rate. In total, 54 participants have completed the entire survey among all 95 respondents.

Conclusion and Next Steps

The present survey was able to bring us insights with regards to the relevance and purpose of knowing user attributes for each department in the company. Based on some of the responses, it is important to emphasise that not every team has a clear understanding of its contribution to the user personalization topic. In this context, further comments and insights will be provided in a google sheet for individual em more critical analysis.
